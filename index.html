🔧 Parts List

SBC:    ZimaBlade 7700 (~$120)
IPMI:   BliKVM v2 PCIe or PiKVM PCIe (~$100)
HDDs:   2× 3.5" SATA (your choice)
Riser:  PCIe x4 flexible riser cable (~$15)
PSU:    12V 5A DC (ZimaBlade's own, powers HDDs too)
HW:     6-32 countersunk × 8, M3 standoffs × 4, M3 screws × 8

📖 How to Edit

Open STEP file in FreeCAD (free) or Fusion 360 to:
  - Adjust hole positions
  - Add/remove vent slots
  - Generate flat pattern for SendCutSend
  - Change dimensions (width/depth/height)

Or edit build_v6.py (CadQuery Python):
  - Change constants at the top
  - Run: python build_v6.py
  - Regenerates STEP/STL/GLB
