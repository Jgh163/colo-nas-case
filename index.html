Complete Assembly

Case + top panel + 2× HDD + Mini ITX + standoffs + 1U cooler. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom.

Layout: HDD1 on bottom (right-side up, back-right), HDD2 suspended from top (upside-down, front-left). No collision at 7.5" width — drives occupy different Z layers.
