🔧 Hardware

  • 4× M3×10mm hex standoffs (motherboard)
  • 8× M3×5mm screws (MB→standoffs, standoffs→bottom)
  • 8-16× 6-32×3/16" flathead countersunk (HDDs)
  • 60mm fan (Noctua NF-A6x15 or similar)
  • Rubber isolation pads under HDDs

📖 How to Edit

FreeCAD (free): Download the STEP file, open in FreeCAD. You can edit dimensions, add holes, modify the case shape. Then export a flat pattern DXF for SendCutSend.

CadQuery (Python): Edit build_v5.py, change constants at the top, run: python build_v5.py to regenerate STEP/STL.
